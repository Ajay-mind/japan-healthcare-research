# Japan–India Healthcare Experience Research Initiative (JHERI)

## About

This independent research initiative explores the healthcare experiences of Japanese expatriates living and working in India.

Our objective is to understand:

- Healthcare accessibility
- Medical communication
- Interpretation support
- Insurance coordination
- Patient experience
- Cross-cultural healthcare challenges
